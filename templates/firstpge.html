What is a Flipbook?

A flipbook is an interactive online publication that simulates the experience of a physical book with realistic page-turning animations. Flipbooks can include interactive elements such as embedded videos, clickable links, animations, and slideshows. They are easy to share through a direct link and provide a more engaging reading experience than a traditional PDF.
